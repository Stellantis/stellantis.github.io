Groupe PSA for developers

Groupe PSA for Developers is the dedicated platform for application developers working with Groupe PSA's connected vehicles. Join us and build the future of connected car together!

Build embedded
web APPs.

Try our webportal framework and build wonderful APPs for your vehicle embedded screen using HTML CSS & JavaScript.  

Remote control
your vehicle.

Remote control your vehicles over Bluetooth or HTTP: open the doors, precondition temperature, honk the horn and much more...

user interface.
